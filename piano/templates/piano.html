Learning Piano

Since February 2016, I have been learning to play the piano.

This was something I had wanted to do for a long, long time, but only after graduation did I find I had both the time and the money to sink into a project like this.

I also decided to chronicle the attempt in video form. I hoped that putting it on public record like this would motivate me to keep practicing and keep learning long past the point when I would have otherwise quit after the initial enthusiasm wore away.

I explain in more detail in the introductory video:

I'm very glad I started this. It's very different from all of my other projects, and it's probably one I enjoy the most as well - usually.

As part of this endeavour, I will be logging my hours spent practicing (see the charts below) and posting the videos here. I am currently intending to post videos after every ten hours of practice.

Finally, I have created a simple web app for practcing learning basic musical notes - partly out of amazement that I couldn't find such a tool (though I'm sure there are if you look hard enough). It can be found here.
